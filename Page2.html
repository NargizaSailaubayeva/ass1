Located on two continents, Asia and Europe, Atyrau has a unique and interesting blend of cultures as it attracts numerous expats from all over the world. Atyrau means 'the place where the river flows into the sea'. As the Ural river divides the city into European and Asian sides, Atyrau is one of two cities in the world to be located on two different continents. Thus it is suggested to start sightseeing of the city by taking photos at Asia and Europe signs.
